2021 - 2023

Master's Degree
Master of Health Sciences

Postgraduate Program Stricto Sensu in Health Sciences at the University of São Francisco

A master's degree in Health Sciences with a focus on metabolomics utilizing UPLC-QTOF-MS involves an in-depth study of human health and biology, emphasizing the analysis of metabolites. In this context, UPLC (Ultra Performance Liquid Chromatography) and QTOF-MS (Quadrupole Time-of-Flight Mass Spectrometry) are state-of-the-art technologies used to analyze and identify metabolites present in biological samples

Metabolomics is a discipline that aims to understand metabolic profiles in biological samples, such as blood, urine, and tissues, to identify health markers, diseases, and the body's responses to different factors. The combination of UPLC, which allows efficient compound separation, and QTOF-MS, providing high-resolution and precise mass analysis, is a powerful approach for identifying metabolites in complex samples.

Students in this field can learn to conduct experiments, process massive data, and interpret results to gain valuable insights into human health. Research in metabolomics has significant applications in areas such as personalized medicine, disease biomarkers, and the discovery of new therapies. Therefore, a master's degree in Health Sciences focusing on metabolomics utilizing UPLC-QTOF-MS prepares students for an engaging career in health research and innovation.
